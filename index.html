Us, in a nutshell

Founded in 2020, Ofek partners with top S&P 100 companies to optimize their manufacturing operations in Taiwan through tailored consultancy services.

Drawing on deep knowledge of Taiwan's supply chain and our engineering expertise, we build strong relationships with local manufacturers, helping partners navigate technical, cultural, and regional challenges for smoother operations.

We complement our consulting with HeatVision®, our proprietary platform that integrates real-time data across every stage of manufacturing, from SMT to RMA. By harnessing big data and machine learning, HeatVision® eliminates silos, anticipates errors, improves yields, and reduces defects, turning complex data into actionable insights.

Our multilingual team supports partners across Latin America, EAMEA, North America, and Asia-Pacific, with fluency in Mandarin, Cantonese, Hebrew, Spanish, Russian, and English.

We're committed to building long-term partnerships based on trust and transparency. We prioritize, always, what's best for our partner, creating lasting value.
